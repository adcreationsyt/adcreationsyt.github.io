Identity

Cognitive Performance

Recites 20 of the longest English words — with full spellings — starting from 25 all the way up to 183 letters, within 150 seconds. Performs them both forward and backwards simultaneously. Also pronounces long numbers from millions to centillion googolplex (303 zeros).

Background

Born January 14, 2015, son of Manoj Das and Divya Satish Naik. His family migrated to the UAE in 2015, where he currently lives near Rolla Mall, Sharjah and studies at Ryan International School.

Digital Creation

Active content creator on YouTube at @AdithyaManojDas2015 and the ADCreations channel, featuring record attempts, award footage, and performance content.

Recognition

Recognized by 16 international record organizations including URF, American, European, British, International, Arabian, and Einstein Books of World Records. Recipient of 3 International Awards including the prestigious Camel International Award (Dubai, 2023).
